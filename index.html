Introducing My Site

My name is Will, and I am going to be a Web Developer. My earlier days I spent most my time playing video games and goofing around with friends. I learned to appreciate and love coffee and shortly after that, food and beer. I have lived in California my whole life, 50% NorCal and 50% SoCal. Weird isn't it? Long story but very true. I've learned a lot working as a bartender, been through my fair share of bad days but all in all, I'm happy with where I am and who I am. My experiences have made me who I am today and I wouldn't change a single thing!... Who am I kidding, I would.

This series is called "The Stormlight Archives". A 10 book series with only 4 books published (the most recent being "Rhythm of War" released at the end of last year) is a post-apocalyptic fantasy story with a myriad of characters, each with their own detailed personality and life; A vast world which, even after all the information you're given in book one barely scratches the surface of its layout and inhabitants. Brandon Sanderson is the "Davinci" of story telling, interweaving and foreshadowing.
